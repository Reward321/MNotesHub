Empowering Students Through Free Education

🚀 Our Story

FreeMBOSE was created with the goal of helping students access organized notes and solved question papers without financial burden. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone.

💡 Why We Built This

Many students struggle to find structured and reliable study material. FreeMBOSE provides chapter-wise notes and exam-focused resources in a clean and distraction-free environment.

💰 How It Is Funded

The platform is independently maintained. In the future, minimal advertisements and optional donations may help support hosting and development costs while keeping the resources free for students.

🛠 Tech Stack

  • HTML5 – Website Structure
  • CSS3 – Modern Responsive Design
  • JavaScript – Search & Interactions
  • Google Drive – PDF Hosting
  • Google Analytics – Visitor Tracking

👤 Founder

FreeMBOSE was founded by a passionate student developer who believes that technology can solve real educational problems. The vision is to create a free, simple and effective learning platform for MBOSE students.

💻 Developer

The website is designed and developed independently using modern web technologies. Continuous improvements are being made to enhance performance, user experience and accessibility.

🌍 Our Vision

Our long-term goal is to expand FreeMBOSE into a complete learning ecosystem including mock tests, AI-powered doubt solving, and a student dashboard.
