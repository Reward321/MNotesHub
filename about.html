🚀 Our Story

It all started when I,Rewardking,a nerd who use to build website get a call from Fosterwell,he is preparing for JEE and he realised that its very hard to get quality note and resources so after talking for hours we finally agree to work together and also bring onboard two of our old classmates and close friend Robert & Rewardmaker,I show them prototype and all of us are excited and we decided to build this together for the benefit of all the student in Meghalaya who struggle to find resources to study

💡 Why We Built This

We saw students struggle to find resources on the internet for free so we build this website for students to download notes,study them and came back to the website and test themselves through MCQ and we also introduced a daily Quiz who those who want to know the major development of the world
